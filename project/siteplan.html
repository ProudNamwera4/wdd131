City Academy Site Plan

CityAcademyFC.com

For this project, I have chosen the site name: CityAcademyFC.com

I chose this site name because it clearly communicates the name and purpose of the soccer/football club, which is to provide a platform for players, parents, and fans to access information, news, and resources related to the club.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the City Academy website is to provide a central hub for information, news, and resources related to the club. The site will serve as a platform for players, parents, and fans to access important information, stay up-to-date on the latest news and events, and connect with the club.

The site will provide the following services and information:

  • Club news and updates
  • Team schedules and scores
  • Player and coach profiles
  • Registration and membership information
  • Contact information and forms

By providing a comprehensive and user-friendly platform, the City Academy website aims to enhance the overall experience for players, parents, and fans, while also promoting the club's mission and values.

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent questions asked by site visitors who are part of the target audience for the City Academy website.

Scenario 1: A parent of a new player is looking for information on how to register their child for the upcoming season.

Question: "What is the registration process for new players, and what are the requirements for joining the team?"

Scenario 2: A fan is looking for information on the upcoming games and events for the team.

Question: "What are the dates and times for the upcoming games, and where can I find the schedule?"

Color Schema

The color schema for the City Academy website has been carefully selected to reflect the club's brand and values.

Primary Color: #3498db (a bright, energetic blue)

  • Headings: The primary color will be used for headings throughout the website, including page titles, section headings, and button text.
  • Accents: The primary color will also be used for accents throughout the website, including buttons, icons, and other interactive elements.

Secondary Color: #f1c40f (a warm, inviting yellow)

  • Background Colors: The secondary color will be used for background colors throughout the website, including page backgrounds, section backgrounds, and other design elements.
  • Highlight Colors: The secondary color will also be used for highlight colors throughout the website,including hover effects, active states, and other interactive elements.

Typography

The typography for the City Academy website has been carefully selected to reflect the club's brand and values.

Primary Font: Open Sans

  • Font Family: Open Sans
  • Font Style: Clean, modern sans-serif font
  • Usage: Body text, paragraphs, and other general content

Secondary Font: Montserrat

  • Font Family: Montserrat
  • Font Style: Bold, geometric sans-serif font
  • Usage: Headings, titles, and other prominent text elements

Accent Font: Great Vibes

  • Font Family: Great Vibes
  • Font Style: Script font with a friendly, cursive feel
  • Usage: Special sections, such as testimonials or quotes, to add a touch of personality and whimsy
